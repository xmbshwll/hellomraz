The Gregg Deal and Lee Tasche project returns with a second album. Traditionally, the strongest Spoken Word lyrics are on the topic of decolonization and hatred of white people in the United States, with an anger very similar to the young Eugene Robinson. Musically it has become even more diverse and assertive, there are flirtations directly with punk hardcore. An even more powerful development of the ideas of one of the best albums of 23rd year.
