40 minutes of some of the most assertive death metal to be heard this year. The description of the Denver album is also just right.

__In order to understand the dark sublimity of this album, maybe other perspectives must be considered, which are not yet defined properly. In our world, it will be considered ugly and oppressive by those who are not able to succumb to its glorious depths. Intentionally breaking with modern listening habits (and avoiding all rules of songlength and structure), it achieves the best result when experienced in darkness and at maximum volume.
__
