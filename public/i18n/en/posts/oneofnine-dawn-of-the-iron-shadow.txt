The second album of a semi-anonymous black metal project interspersed with symphonic sounds, completely involved in the mythology of Tolkien and his Lord of the Rings. The team has grown noticeably by the second recording, and at this rate one can expect something very interesting and less conventional on subsequent releases.

BON DETECTOR

Life experience shows that almost all lovers of Tolkien’s works turn out to be ultra-zygametes in reality, so it’s dangerous. But if you are generally satisfied with apartheid in relation to Mordor and its inhabitants, then why not listen.
