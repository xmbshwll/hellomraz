Posthumous album by a veteran of the British music scene, member of the cult The Pop Group, classmate of Nick Sheppard from The Clash, owner of collaborations with Reznor, Tricky, and so on. A somewhat disjointed collection, due to its raggedness, but thoroughly imbued with the spirit of a pioneer of the scene - from ballads in the spirit of Nick Cave to dark synth pop bangers with Youth from Killing Joke on production.
