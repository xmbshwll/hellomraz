Genghis Tron returned after 5 years with a new album and the addition of Kenny Szymanski (The Armed) and Nick Yacyshyn (Sumac, Baptists) to the lineup. As is tradition among experimenters, the sound has changed, more electronics have returned, kraut and prog rock have appeared. In places it sounds like a calmer and less chaotic version of The Armed. The longplay does not make the same impression as Dream Weapon of 21, but it seems that this was not the goal.
