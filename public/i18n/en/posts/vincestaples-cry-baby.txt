A surprise release from Vince Staples and perhaps the most interesting in his discography. The sound has changed - live raw instruments, flirting with electropunk. This is backed by a traditionally strong textual component, which only enhances the uncomfortable feeling - here is a song comparing chasing a criminal to catching a gorilla, and a caustic satire on the political slogans of the movement magician and the violence that accompanies them, and a track about the Middle East conflict.
