FUCKED HOTEL - A GLASS OF POISON (2025)

The second album by the outstanding, but undeservedly unpopular, young five-piece from Portsmouth, subjectively one of the most interesting young British guitar groups of recent times, along with Murder Capital. Written entirely in a local pub and generally putting the British musical tradition of pub rock on a modern footing. The record itself is also conceptual - about the death of working-class neighborhoods in Britain in the 20th century.

The name of the group also deserves attention and is dedicated to the Central Hotel on Tverskaya Street in Moscow, which at one time was called the Lux Hotel.

__After the October Revolution of 1917, the hotel was nationalized and named “Lux”. They began to systematically house communists from different countries who arrived in Moscow for Comintern congresses, as well as communist political emigrants. The largest group among the hotel residents were Germans, among whom were such later famous political figures as Walter Ulbricht, Wilhelm Pieck, Ernst Thälmann, Herbert Wehner, Ernst Reuter. The intelligence officer Richard Sorge also lived in this hotel. Zhou Enlai and Ho Chi Minh lived at the hotel.

In 1934 it was built on. The reconstruction project was carried out by the architect B. Ya. Ulinich.

During the Great Terror of 1937-1938, two thirds of communist emigrants from Germany living in the USSR were repressed. Of the 1,400 leading German communists in the USSR, 178 died during the Great Terror (almost all of them lived in the Lux Hotel).

The German communists who remained in the Lux Hotel were evacuated to Ufa in the fall of 1941 and settled in the hotel again in 1942. They lived in a hotel until 1945, when they were able to return to their homeland.__
