And another highly anticipated pop release - the fourth album from twin sisters Naomi and Lisa-Kaindé Diaz. The duo left XL Recordings, becoming independent, and, it seems, have finally gained complete creative freedom - there is not such an insane number of collaborations as on the previous album Spell 31 of 2022. The noticeable influences of the sisters' father (a Cuban-born percussionist) and growing up in Paris are woven into a unique, very beautiful sound.
