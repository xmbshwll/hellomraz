A highly anticipated debut. The Londoner, of Egyptian-Ethiopian origin, with the widest musical range, is finally releasing a solo album. 15 extremely diverse non-genre compositions, which have everything - soul, breakbeat, homages to early M.I.A, nods to Ethiopian folk and local percussion. The debut was supported by a number of people - eejebee (drummer Little Simz) on percussion, on the mix - Grammy winners who were once behind Sugababes and Rizzle Kicks, producer - Shy FX, a legend of the drum'n'bass scene.
