Good evening! Today I was filled with nostalgia and there will be a post about the design for the merch of my latest band totoro. Back in 2016, we released an album and it seems that after that we decided to make a small edition of T-shirts. Then I tried to do everything by hand as much as possible - sometime later I will show a disc with the above-mentioned album. And so for the T-shirts, I came up with the idea of ​​writing all the lyrics of the album in a row without spaces or punctuation marks in order to achieve texture and put a logo on top, which we essentially never had, but just a handwritten inscription. This whole thing was written with a brush and ink. I translated quite a decent amount of paper. But in the end we got an original print based on lettering. I always remember this period of my life with warmth.
