A schoolboy in 1984 records a hit melody on cassette that was played on radio in Hamburg and Lower Saxony. Many years later, his sister in 2007 decides to ask the Internet if anyone knows the author of the composition. After 17 years of searching (which even included going to the Stasi archive, since during the Wall the GDR recorded all West German radio broadcasts), the authors were accidentally found in 2024, and the incredibly sweet story was called “The Most Mysterious Song on the Internet.”

The band from Kiel performed at a talent show in Bremen in the 80s, then broke up and had no idea that the track was being searched for all over the Internet. As a result, after 40 years, the band reunites and records their debut album this summer.

We dance to the rhythm of the golden era of German synthpop.
