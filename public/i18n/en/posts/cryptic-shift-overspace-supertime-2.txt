Sci-fi and progressive thrash metal lovers from Leeds with their second album. The debut long-play in 2020 opened with a 26-minute track, now we have almost a 30-minute track, so logically I think we can expect an hour-long intro for the next album. Particular attention to the varied and extremely witty solos, each of which has a special name - a detailed description in the lyrics on BC for the corresponding track.
