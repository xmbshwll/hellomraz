WORM - PALACE OF THE SCARY NECROMANCER (2026)

The Florida “necromantic black metal” project has returned in its symphonic form - now we have songs about vampires, castles, the suffering of pathetic leather bags and other cool things, along with a synth backing as if from the soundtrack to the next Castlevania. One of the most interesting bands in the American underground metal scene did not disappoint again.
