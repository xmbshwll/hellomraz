New album from the solo noise project of Max Klebanov, a multi-instrumentalist known to the general public as the vocalist and drummer of Tomb Mold. From release to release, the boundaries and shape of the project's sound changed and now we are moving from harsh to an interesting combination of ambient and field recording. The album, in a sense, is a soundtrack to the process of recording the album itself - we hear the movement of various objects in the studio, grinding and attempts to find a new sound. A hypnotic recording, the best in the Death Kneel catalogue.
