Personal favorites and also big fans of sludge and frogs - with the new, fifth album in the cycle about the KING OF THE FROGS.

We find Herman deep in the ancient forest; 10 years after the events of SONS OF THE FROG KING and another 490 before THE ASCENSION OF THE AMPHIBIAN, spending this time in deep amphibious meditation. Visions of a meteor, whispers of prophecy, rumors of a swamp. Herman the Man must soon embark on a journey to complete his metamorphosis and become the GREAT TOAD WIZARD; spiritual mentor of the KING OF THE FROGS.
