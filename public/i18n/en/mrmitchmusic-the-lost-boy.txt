Well, as they say, not a word about grime - one of the main producers of the second wave of grime, who often worked with Skepta in particular, with a very soulful and diverse fusion of British electronics on a solo album - there is soul, pop, trance, house, and grime itself and echoes of early dubstep. All with Foggy Albion's signature smoky mastering and very bright bass. Great album.
