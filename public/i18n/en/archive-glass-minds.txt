Archive are back with a new release, after re-releasing their entire classic discography. Only one song is shorter than 5 minutes (and that one is 4:58), the opening track offers a constant build-up of atmosphere without any climax, a dystopian atmosphere and political commentary on the spot. The album, which sounds like a logical continuation of Controlling Crowds, may not be the best in the band's outstanding discography - but it is worthy.
