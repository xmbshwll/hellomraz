The 12th album by the Berliner of Swedish origin, a big fan of the 80s and synthpop. Traditionally, the work is conceptual, but this time we move further away from dark themes (the previous album was indirectly dedicated to the atrocities of the NSDAP regime) and get a life-affirming record about what is understandable to all fans of punk rock. There is no greater enemy than professionalism. It's cool to be an amateur!
