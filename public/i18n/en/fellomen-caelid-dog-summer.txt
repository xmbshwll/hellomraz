An interesting punk black metal release from Greece, from a semi-anonymous multi-instrumentalist. Not only is UK'82's extensive influence on the sound noteworthy, but the theme of the lyrics is metal about Elden Ring. The editors of the world's best music-critical media outlet, Put Down Your Guns, Scum are completely psychologically healthy and are not carried away by Miyazaki's endless projects, but the music is good and in isolation from the references to the shadowy Japanese genius.
