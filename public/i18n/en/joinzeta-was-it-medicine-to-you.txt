A new, first English-speaking album in the discography, an album by the Venezuelan five with a title in the form of the sixth letter of the Greek alphabet, which became a symbol of protests as a result of the murder of Grigoris Lambrakis by the regime of black colonels in 1963.

Post-hardcore with a touch of At The Drive-In, featuring a feature with Mani Nikka from Soul Glo and Anthony Green from Circa Survive.
