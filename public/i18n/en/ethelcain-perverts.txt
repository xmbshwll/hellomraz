Quite unexpected in form, minimalistic, cold and near-drone album from the American queen of gothic pop. Traditionally conceptual work, ideologically and thematically related to the breakthrough Preacher's Daughter of the 22nd year. A lot has been written about the phenomenon of Ethel and her complex biography, but for those who want to get acquainted, it’s easier to start by reading her tumblr (yes, she still runs tumblr)
