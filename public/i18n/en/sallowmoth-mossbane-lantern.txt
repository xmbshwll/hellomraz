New album from Dallas metal prodigy Garry Brents (a total of about 50 releases over the past 5 years, scattered across his galaxy of projects - Sallow Moth, Gonemage, Memorrhage, Homeskin, and so on). Despite the fecundity that King Gizzard could envy, the quality bar is always maintained.

This time a new album as part of a project dedicated to the Magic: The Gathering universe. It's a hoe, but musically it's a dynamic and constantly changing mixture of grind and death metal, you won't get bored for a second.
