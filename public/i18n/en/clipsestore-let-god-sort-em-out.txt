16 years after the previous album - the return of brothers Pusha T, No Malice with production from Pharell. A bunch of famous hits and a change of topic, which is unexpected from Pusha T, who spent his entire life developing the formula for the perfect track about being a huckster, and seems to have reached perfection on Diet Coke with the punch "you wanted diet coke? that's a joke, right?"

A top-notch rap album and a rare chance to hear Pusha T rapping about substances.
