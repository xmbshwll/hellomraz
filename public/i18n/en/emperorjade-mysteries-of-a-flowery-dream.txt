The third album of good atmospheric death metallers, flirting a little with dissonance. The four are notable for the fact that they are from a region that is not very typical for the genre - Catalani, and that from their stage names one can almost make up the abbreviation A.C.A.B. According to the texts, there is something about stars, space spiders, Mayan calendars - everything is fine according to the texts, too, in short.
