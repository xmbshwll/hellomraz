The first release of the (now Berlin) legends after leaving Spotifty is an album of reinterpretations of songs by other artists, apparently the first in a series. As always with Jamie and company - very sensual and good. I caught myself thinking that I would love to listen to Xiu Xiu’s cover of classic hardcore bangers for some reason, I wouldn’t even refuse the album’s cover of Songs To Scream At The Sun.
