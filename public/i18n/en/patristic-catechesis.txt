In accordance with the famous meme about thoughts about the Roman Empire - the long-play debut of blackened death metallers from Italy, a side project of the guitarist of Hideous Divinity. In contrast to the expected theme in this case, the group is dedicated not to right-wing nostalgia about the great empire, but to catechesis and specifically its process in the disintegrating Roman Empire - a period when the pagan faith was gradually replaced by Christianity.
