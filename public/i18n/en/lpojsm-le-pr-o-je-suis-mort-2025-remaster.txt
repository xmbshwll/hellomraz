Adapted for fans of French screamo: the band Le Pré Où Je Suis Mort has acquired a bandcamp, which contains a remastered version of their EP of the same name, released in 2008.
The release was also uploaded to streaming services for the first time.

Fan fact: I just found out that this group is not from France at all, but from Switzerland, specifically Geneva.
