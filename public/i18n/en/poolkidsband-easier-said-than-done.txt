The heroes of the latest wave of emo revived with their third album, now signed to the major label Epitaph. Lots of good songwriting and lyrics, more mainstream standard sound and much less of the signature features of the early albums.

I like to think that the title of the long play is the band’s own response to the title of their debut album “Music to Practice Safe Sex to”.

And yes, I hope the hardcore side project POOL will also be released on Epitaph.
