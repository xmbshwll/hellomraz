Oliver Krabs, the vocalist of the important German emo band Yage, who played in the late 90s and early 00s, has died.

In 2012, he gave an interview to the Russian-language blog catchingupmyself. Answering the question “What do you think Yage left behind?”, he wrote the following:

I can't say what people think about Yage, you know best. But we must realize that we do not have such a long period of time to live. I think the meaning of life is that when you look back you feel happy. And thinking about Yage makes me really happy.
