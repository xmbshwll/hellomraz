While music critics and the public are seriously discussing the new product of the machine for producing sterile, meaningless, stylized pop hits - the real American Heart, instead of Benson Boone, for the last two years, it seems, is Jess Wells.

The former vocalist of the good psyrock bands Dead Indian and Cosmo-American began a new chapter of his solo career two years ago with short protest neofolk sketches on current political topics on social networks. Quickly becoming the people's voice of America (and, if you listen to the lyrics, the people's voice of the entire working class), going from tik-tok to appearances on the Jimmy Kimmel show - Welles and his career amusingly rhyme with Benson Boone here too.

A musical continuation of the powerful American tradition of guitar folk/dark country, crossed with very witty and caustic left-wing populist lyrics - a wonderfully working mixture. Perhaps a new Bob Dylan/Johnny Cash is growing before our eyes.

Latest album at the moment.
