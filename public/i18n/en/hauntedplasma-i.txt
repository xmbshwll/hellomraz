A powerful debut from the super trio - Timo Kaukolampi (K-X-P), Tomi Leppänen (Circle) and Juho Vanhanen (Oranssi Pazuzu). As one might expect from these people - a poorly defined genre cocktail, jumping from synthwave, in the spirit of 80s sci-fi cinema soundtracks, to synth pop and avant rock, jumping over black and post-rock along the way. There's even vocals via a vocoder, how long have you heard that?
